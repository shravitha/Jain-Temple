Why 1000 pillar

History & Design: The Thousand Pillars Temple was built in the 15th century by local ruler Devaraya Wadiyar. Temple was renovated in 1962. Each of the thousand pillars is dotted with exquisite rock carvings.

About swamiji

Sri Sri Swasthisri Charukeerthi Bhattaraka Pandithacharayavariya Swamiji of Jain Math, Moodbidri is a well-known spiritual figure among all communities. He has organised many religious meets and Savardharama Sammelanas.
